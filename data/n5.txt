Verb Conjugation Basics
ます形 (ますけい, Masu form): The polite form of verbs.
て形 (てけい, Te form): Used to connect clauses or for requests.
た形 (たけい, Ta form): The past tense form.
ない形 (ないけい, Nai form): The negative form.
Sentence Endings
です (Desu): Polite ending for non-verbal sentences.
ですか (Desu ka): Polite question ending.
でしょう (Deshou): Expressing probability or asking for confirmation (polite).
ですね (Desu ne): Seeking agreement or confirmation.
Particles
は (Wa): Topic marker.
が (Ga): Subject marker.
を (O): Direct object marker.
に (Ni): Indicates direction, time, or indirect object.
へ (E): Indicates direction.
で (De): Indicates the location of an action or means.
から (Kara): Starting point (from).
まで (Made): Ending point (until).
と (To): Used to connect nouns (and), with.
や (Ya): Used to list multiple items (and so on).
の (No): Possessive particle (of).
も (Mo): Also, too.
ね (Ne): Seeks agreement (isn't it?).
よ (Yo): Emphasizing information (you know).
Basic Grammar Structures
です (Desu): Polite ending for nouns and adjectives.
じゃありません (Ja arimasen): Negative form of "desu."
でした (Deshita): Past form of "desu."
じゃありませんでした (Ja arimasen deshita): Past negative form of "desu."
Verbs and Adjectives
い-Adjectives: Adjectives ending in い (e.g., あつい, atsui - hot).
な-Adjectives: Adjectives followed by な before a noun (e.g., きれいな, kirei na - beautiful).
い-adjective conjugation: Present affirmative (あついです), present negative (あつくないです), past affirmative (あつかったです), past negative (あつくなかったです).
な-adjective conjugation: Present affirmative (きれいです), present negative (きれいじゃありません), past affirmative (きれいでした), past negative (きれいじゃありませんでした).
Requests and Commands
てください (Te kudasai): Please do.
ないでください (Naide kudasai): Please don't do.
ましょう (Mashou): Let's do.
ましょうか (Mashou ka): Shall we do?
Describing
あります (Arimasu) / います (Imasu): There is/are (inanimate/animate).
が好きです (Ga suki desu): Like something.
がきらいです (Ga kirai desu): Dislike something.
がほしいです (Ga hoshii desu): Want something.
Time and Frequency
とき (Toki): When.
前に (Mae ni): Before.
あとで (Ato de): After.
から (Kara): Since, from.
まで (Made): Until.
もう (Mou): Already.
まだ (Mada): Still, yet.
Comparisons
より (Yori): More than.
のほうが (No hou ga): More, prefer.
Additional Structures
から (Kara) and ので (Node): Because, since.
と (To): If, when (conditional).
けど (Kedo): But, although.
でも (Demo): But, however.
